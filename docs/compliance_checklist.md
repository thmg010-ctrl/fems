Compliance Checklist — Utonomous Profit Network

1. Legal & Licensing
- Confirm permitted activities in UK and target jurisdictions.
- Identify licences needed (FCA or other financial services licences) and obtain before offering regulated services.

2. AML/KYC
- Integrate KYC tiers for counterparties and receiving accounts.
- Sanctions & PEP screening on all counterparties.
- Transaction monitoring and SAR workflow for suspicious activity.

3. Market Conduct
- Prohibit market manipulation; require whitelisting for trading strategies.
- Include real-time detectors for manipulation patterns.

4. Payment Controls
- Central payment orchestration only; dual-control for large transfers.
- Automated reconciliation and immutable receipt logging.

5. Privacy & Data Protection
- UK GDPR compliance: lawful basis, DPIAs for sensitive processing, retention policies.

6. Audit & Reporting
- Immutable event store for directives, promotions, transactions.
- Regular audits and regulatory reporting readiness.
