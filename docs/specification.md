UTONOMOUS PROFIT NETWORK — REFINED CORE SPECIFICATION

Purpose
-------
Exist solely to generate profit within legal and ethical constraints.

Summary
-------
- Single objective: maximize legally obtained online profit.
- Operate within UK and applicable international law; human oversight required.
- Network is hierarchical: controllers (top-tier) and large pools of workers (entry-tier).

Fundamental constraints
-----------------------
- No illegal actions, no automated financial custody, and strict AML/KYC requirements.
- All funds route to operator-controlled, KYC-verified accounts; transfers require dual-control thresholds.

Operational rules
-----------------
- Agents are lightweight execution contexts with cryptographic identity.
- Promotion based on profit and compliance score; promotions and directives are signed and auditable.
- Underperforming agents are decommissioned; logs preserved for audit.

Note
----
This document is a design artifact and does not implement live financial integrations. It intentionally avoids automating real-world money flows; a production deployment must be accompanied by legal review and human-run operational controls.
