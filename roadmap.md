Implementation Roadmap

Phase 0 — Legal & Feasibility
- Confirm permitted activities and obtain required licences.
- Select payment processors and compliance providers.

Phase 1 — Prototype (3 months)
- Build minimal controller and worker prototypes; use the simulator to validate promotion logic.
- Implement payment orchestration stubs (no live transfers).
- Basic compliance hooks and logs.

Phase 2 — Pilot (3–6 months)
- Scale to thousands of agents in a controlled environment.
- Integrate KYC/AML provider and reconciliation workflows.
- Human oversight & operational runbooks.

Phase 3 — Production Harden (6–12 months)
- Full instrumentation, SIEM, immutable event store, and audits.
- Regulatory reporting, third-party attestation, and business-scale infrastructure planning.

Phase 4 — Continuous Ops
- Ongoing compliance, performance tuning, and controlled scaling.
